Consistency Over Intensity

Regular moderate exercise beats sporadic intense workouts. Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly to build lasting habits and see sustainable results.

üíß

Stay Hydrated

Proper hydration enhances performance and recovery. Drink water before, during, and after workouts. Aim for 8-10 glasses daily, more if you're active.

Prioritize Recovery

Quality sleep is when your body repairs and grows stronger. Aim for 7-9 hours nightly and include rest days in your workout schedule for optimal results.

Set SMART Goals

Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound goals keep you motivated and on track. Break large goals into smaller milestones.

Progressive Overload

Gradually increase workout intensity, duration, or resistance to continue challenging your body and prevent plateaus in your fitness journey.

Mind-Body Connection

Focus on form and breathing during exercises. This improves effectiveness, reduces injury risk, and enhances the mental benefits of physical activity.

  • Choose complex carbohydrates for sustained energy
